About Me

My name is Daniel Sainz. I live in Alajuela, Costa Rica. I studied digital animation at Universidad Veritas. Why digital animation? Well, since I was a child I´ve been impressed about art, because it lets you create any world you can imagine. That’s what I try to show in my projects.

I am currently developing myself as an illustrator, character animator and character and environment designer, in both 2D and 3D.

I worked in an animated short film called “Hamburguesa de pescado” which participated in several international festivals. Likewise I worked in the production of another short film in order to sensitize people about environmental damage. I´ve also animated commercials for some important brands in the country such as Tang and Choys.

Feel free to contact me.

My Services

2D

I love to design characters and environments, most of my work is digital but I also enjoy traditional drawing and inking.

3D

My skills in 3D focus on character animation, modeling and sculpting both, low or high poly models.

Animation

One of my passions is to animate 3D characters, but I also enjoy to animate in 2D software like Flash or after effects.

Post Production

I am not a postproduction lover but I do enjoy color correction and camera projection in software like after effects.
